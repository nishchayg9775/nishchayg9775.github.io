01 Origin · Print discipline

Print taught me to ship.

I started with book covers in Agra—200+ of them, with print-ready artwork across 100+ titles. I walked in as an intern and was promoted twice before I left. Print teaches you one thing fast: there is no version two after it ships.

02 Now · Performance pace

Every frame has a job.

Now I'm the senior graphic designer at Univest: 250+ performance creatives across 40+ campaign concepts and the full funnel after the click. Before that, I designed 35+ investor decks and identities for 10+ fintech products at Payomatix.

03 Method · Measured judgement

Taste leads. Data decides.

My degree is in mathematics, and it shows in how I work. I ship A/B variants and let the numbers settle the argument. Midjourney, Firefly and ChatGPT accelerate exploration, but AI stays upstream—it never gets the final say on a frame.
