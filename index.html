05 — Process

Taste, with a method.

  1. Step 01

    Understand

    I read the brief, the data and the audience before I open Figma. No design yet. The problem shapes the creative, not the other way around.

  2. Step 02

    Concept

    Directions explored wide with Midjourney, Firefly and ChatGPT, then narrowed by hand. AI gives me speed at the ideation stage. The judgement stays mine.

  3. Step 03

    Design

    The chosen route becomes finished creative in Figma, Photoshop or Illustrator. Built as a system, so the tenth variant looks like it belongs with the first.

  4. Step 04

    Test

    Campaigns ship in A/B variations. The audience votes with clicks, and I don't argue with the audience.

  5. Step 05

    Iterate

    Winners get scaled, losers get studied. That loop is how 250+ creatives at Univest turned into 40+ campaign concepts that kept getting sharper.
